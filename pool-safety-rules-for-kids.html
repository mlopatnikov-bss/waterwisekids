Pool Safety Rules for Kids

Teaching children clear pool safety rules helps reduce risks and encourages responsible behavior around water.

Always Swim with an Adult Watching

Children should never swim without supervision. A responsible adult should always be watching the pool when kids are in the water.

Walk, Don’t Run

Pool decks can be slippery. Walking carefully helps prevent falls and injuries.

Listen to Lifeguards and Instructors

Lifeguards and swim instructors provide rules to keep everyone safe. Children should always follow their directions.

Learn Basic Swimming Skills

Swimming lessons help children develop important abilities such as floating and reaching the wall.

Learn more about floating in our guide on how to teach a child to float.

Never Swim Alone

Even strong swimmers should never swim by themselves.

Parents who want children to gain confidence safely may want to explore professional instruction through our swim lesson directory.
