Pool Safety Rules for Kids

Children should learn clear pool safety rules as early as possible. Simple habits can make a big difference in helping kids behave more safely around water.

Always Swim with an Adult Watching

Children should never enter the water without a responsible adult actively supervising them.

Walk, Don’t Run

Pool decks can be slippery. Running increases the risk of falls and injuries.

Ask Permission Before Entering the Water

Children should understand that they must never get into a pool unless an adult says it is okay.

Listen to Lifeguards and Instructors

Adults in charge are there to keep everyone safe. Children should follow directions immediately.

Do Not Push or Roughhouse

Pushing, dunking, and horseplay can quickly turn dangerous around water.

Learn the Wall and Exit Points

Even young swimmers benefit from knowing where the edge is and how to get out safely.
