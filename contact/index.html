Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical response time?
We aim to respond to all messages within 24 business hours. During peak periods, it may take up to 48 hours. For urgent matters, please call or email us directly.
How do I add my swim school to the directory?
Visit our Add Your School page to submit your information. We offer free basic listings and optional paid featured listings for schools wanting more visibility.
How do I advertise on WaterWiseKids?
We offer several advertising packages tailored to swim schools, instructors, and aquatics businesses. Visit our Advertise page to learn about options and pricing, or contact us for a custom package.
Can I post a job opening?
Yes! Our jobs board is free for individual postings or affordable for unlimited postings. Visit post a job to get started.
Do you accept article suggestions?
Absolutely. We're always looking for ideas to improve our water safety education. Send us your suggestions via the contact form above with "Editorial / Article Suggestion" as the subject.
