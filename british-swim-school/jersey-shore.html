For years, Jersey Shore families in Monmouth and Ocean Counties have trusted British Swim School for expert, safety-first swim instruction. Frequently Asked Questions About British Swim School Jersey Shore

How do I get started?

Contact us to schedule a free consultation where we'll assess your child's needs and discuss the best program. You'll also receive a complimentary trial lesson so your child can experience our instruction before committing.

What makes your instructors special?

All our instructors hold current certifications from Red Cross and/or Aquatics Council. They receive ongoing training in safety, development, and teaching techniques. Beyond credentials, they're passionate about water safety and genuinely care about each student's progress.

Do you teach ocean/beach safety?

Yes. Our instructors understand Jersey Shore conditions and include ocean safety awareness in our curriculum. We teach about currents, tides, and open-water safety alongside swimming skills.

What if my child is afraid of water?

We work with water-anxious children every day. Our patient, certified instructors create safe, supportive environments where every child progresses at their own pace. Many of our most confident swimmers started as fearful kids!

Are classes really year-round?

Yes. Our heated indoor pools allow year-round instruction. This consistency is excellent for skill development and ensures you don't have seasonal gaps in your child's progress.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes. We offer private lessons for families preferring one-on-one instruction or with specific goals. Contact us to discuss options and availability.
