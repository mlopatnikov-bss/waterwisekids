Welcome to British Swim School of Northwest Philadelphia

For years, families across Philadelphia, Cheltenham, Glenside, Ambler, and surrounding communities have trusted British Swim School to provide expert, safety-first swim instruction. We specialize in beginner-friendly lessons for infants as young as 6 months through adults, with flexible scheduling and proven curriculum.

Our small class sizes, heated indoor pools, and certified instructors ensure every student receives personalized attention and develops water confidence at their own pace.

Frequently Asked Questions About British Swim School

How do I get started with lessons?

We offer a free consultation where we'll assess your child's needs and discuss which program is best. You'll also get a complimentary trial lesson so your child can experience our instruction before committing. Simply contact us to schedule your free consultation.

What are your class sizes?

We maintain small class sizes of 4-6 students per certified instructor for optimal individual attention. This ratio ensures every student gets personalized feedback and progresses at their own pace.

How long has British Swim School been teaching?

With years of experience and thousands of successful students, British Swim School has built a reputation for excellence in water safety and swim instruction. We're a trusted partner for families throughout the Philadelphia region.

Are instructors certified?

Yes. All our instructors hold current certifications from the Red Cross and/or Aquatics Council. They receive ongoing training to maintain the highest standards of safety and instruction.

Do you offer makeup classes or private lessons?

Yes. We understand life happens. Contact us about makeup options and private lesson availability. We work with families to ensure they get consistent instruction.

What if my child has special needs or fears water?

We love working with all children, including those with special needs or water anxiety. Our patient, certified instructors create safe, supportive environments where every child can progress at their own pace. Contact us to discuss your child's specific situation.
