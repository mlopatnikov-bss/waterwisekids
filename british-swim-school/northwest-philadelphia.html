British Swim School of Northwest Philadelphia

Safety-First Swim Instruction for Infants Through Adults

Welcome to British Swim School of Northwest Philadelphia

For years, families across Philadelphia, Cheltenham, Glenside, Ambler, and surrounding communities have trusted British Swim School to provide expert, safety-first swim instruction. We specialize in beginner-friendly lessons for infants as young as 6 months through adults, with flexible scheduling and a proven curriculum rooted in drowning prevention and water survival skills.

Our small class sizes, heated indoor pools, and certified instructors ensure every student receives personalized attention and develops water confidence at their own pace. Not sure when to start lessons? We can help you figure out the right time for your family.

Programs for Every Age

6 months - 3 years

Infant & Toddler

Parent-child classes focusing on water comfort, floating, and submersion safety. A safe first step into the water with a caring parent present. Learn more about toddler water safety.

3 - 4 years

Pre-School

Independent water exploration with instructor guidance. Building water confidence through play-based learning. See our guide to swim milestones by age.

4 - 5 years

Preschool+

Developing fundamental strokes and water safety awareness. Kids learn to propel themselves and gain real independence in the water.

6+ years

School-Age

Comprehensive stroke development, diving basics, and water survival skills. Building toward independent swimming mastery and strong pool safety habits.

Teens & Adults

Advanced Programs

Technique refinement, competitive training, and specialized instruction. Whether you're a teen building open-water judgment or an adult learning to swim, we have a program for you.

All Ages

Family Classes

Special programming for families wanting to learn together. Build water confidence as a unit and create lasting memories. Read about what to expect at your first lesson.

Frequently Asked Questions About British Swim School

How do I get started with lessons?

We offer a free consultation where we'll assess your child's needs and discuss which program is best. You'll also get a complimentary trial lesson so your child can experience our instruction before committing. Read our guide on what to expect at the first swim lesson, then contact us or email waterwisekids.com@gmail.com to schedule.

What are your class sizes?

We maintain small class sizes of 4-6 students per certified instructor for optimal individual attention. This ratio ensures every student gets personalized feedback and progresses at their own pace.

How long has British Swim School been teaching?

British Swim School is a nationally recognized swim program with a proven curriculum focused on water safety and survival skills. Our Northwest Philadelphia location serves families throughout the greater Philadelphia region with certified, experienced instructors.

Are instructors certified?

Yes. All our instructors hold current certifications from the Red Cross and/or Aquatics Council. They receive ongoing training to maintain the highest standards of safety and instruction.

Do you offer makeup classes or private lessons?

Yes. We understand life happens. Contact us at waterwisekids.com@gmail.com about makeup options and private lesson availability. We work with families to ensure they get consistent instruction.

What if my child has special needs or fears water?

We love working with all children, including those with special needs or water anxiety. Our patient, certified instructors create safe, supportive environments where every child can progress at their own pace. Read our guides on helping children overcome fear of water and water safety for children with special needs, then contact us to discuss your child's specific situation.
