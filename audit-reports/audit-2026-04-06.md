## 🔧 Recommended Fixes

1. **Decide on canonical design tokens.** The audit spec says Arial/#13304a/#f4f8fb, but the live site consistently uses Inter/#1f2937/#eff8ff. Either update the design spec to match reality or update all pages to match the spec. Given the site has been live with Inter for months, updating the spec may be the right call.

2. **Link main.css from all pages.** `assets/css/main.css` exists but is orphaned — no page loads it. Migrating inline styles to the shared stylesheet would enforce consistency and reduce page weight.

3. **Standardize button styling.** Some pages use `border-radius: 999px` pill buttons while hub pages don't. Pick one and apply site-wide.

4. **Add GTM to legacy redirect pages (optional).** The 12 redirect stubs lack GTM. Since they are `noindex` and redirect immediately, impact is negligible, but adding GTM would provide complete redirect tracking.

5. **Consider creating missing hub pages.** The site structure originally planned for `gear.html`, `teens.html`, `jobs.html`, `advertise.html`, and `swim-schools.html`. If these are still on the roadmap, they should be built. If not, remove them from the audit spec.
