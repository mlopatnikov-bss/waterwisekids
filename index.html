Why Water Safety Matters

Drowning is one of the leading causes of accidental death for young children. Learning water survival skills early helps keep kids safe.

When Should Kids Start Swim Lessons?

Experts recommend starting water safety education as early as 6-12 months old to build comfort and confidence in water.
