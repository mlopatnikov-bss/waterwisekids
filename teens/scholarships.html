Swimming Scholarships for Teens

Discover how to fund your swimming journey with competitive scholarships and grants available to talented young swimmers.

College Swimming Scholarships

Many colleges offer full or partial scholarships for competitive swimmers. Requirements typically include:

  • Competitive swimming experience (club or high school team)
  • Qualifying times in your events
  • Good academic standing (GPA 3.0+)
  • Strong character and leadership

NCAA Division I, II, and III

Major colleges compete in NCAA swimming. Scholarships range from full tuition to partial awards. Process begins freshman year of high school.

Club Swim Team Scholarships

Some elite club teams offer scholarships based on financial need and competitive level. Contact local clubs for availability.

Community College Swimming

JUCO swimming offers more scholarships, easier path to D1/D2 schools. Good option if GPA or times aren't quite Division I level yet.

Financial Aid & Grants

  • Swimming Foundation grants for talented swimmers with financial need
  • USA Swimming national and regional scholarship programs
  • State-specific aquatics education grants
  • Private foundation scholarships for swimmers

Getting Recruited

Start early! Build your profile through competitive swimming, attend recruiting showcases, communicate with coaches, and maintain strong academics.
