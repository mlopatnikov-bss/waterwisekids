Get Your Swim School in Front of Local Families

WaterWiseKids helps families find quality swim programs. We'll build you a dedicated profile page, feature your school on our Find Lessons directory, and drive traffic through our water safety education content.

What Partners Get

A full-page profile featuring your programs by age group, service areas, instructor credentials, FAQs, and direct contact paths. See an example: British Swim School of NW Philadelphia.

Featured on Find Lessons

Your school appears on our Find Lessons directory and homepage — the pages families land on when searching for local swim instruction.

Traffic From Our Content

Our education library currently has 43 in-depth articles on topics parents actively search — drowning prevention, when to start lessons, choosing a school. Every article links to partner profiles, turning readers into inquiries.

Post Jobs on Our Aquatic Jobs Board

Need swim instructors or lifeguards? Post open positions on our Aquatic Jobs Board — a free tool built for the aquatics industry to connect schools with qualified candidates.

Built by Swim Instructors

Common Questions

What does it cost?

Nothing right now. Founding partners get listed at no cost. As the platform grows, we may introduce premium placement or lead-tracking features — but your founding profile stays active.

Do we need to be part of a specific franchise?

No. We partner with any quality swim school that meets basic safety and certification standards. The platform is open to all qualified programs — independent schools, franchises, and community programs alike.

What does "founding partner" mean?

It means you're among the first schools on the platform. You get prominent placement, input on features, and your profile stays listed as the site grows — you're grandfathered in.

How do families reach us through the site?

Your profile page includes your phone number, website link, and any booking links you provide. Families contact you directly — we don't sit in the middle.

How quickly can we get listed?

Most profiles go live within a week of initial contact. We'll need your program details, service areas, and any photos or credentials you'd like included.

Become a Founding Partner

We're selectively adding swim schools as founding partners. That means prominent, permanent placement on the platform as it grows — and a voice in how we build it.

No cost to get started. Send us a quick email with your school name, location, and what programs you offer. We'll follow up within two business days to discuss your profile.
