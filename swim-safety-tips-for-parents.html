Swim Safety Tips for Parents

Parents have the biggest influence on how safely children behave around water. Good swim safety habits can reduce risk and help children grow up with healthier respect for water.

Use Active Supervision

When children are near water, adults should stay close, stay focused, and avoid distractions.

Teach Clear Rules

Children should learn not to enter the water without permission and not to swim without an adult present.

Use Pool Barriers and Safety Features

Fences, gates, alarms, and covers help create important layers of protection around pools.

Enroll Children in Swim Lessons

Swim instruction can help children become more comfortable in the water while learning important foundational skills.

Be Prepared for Emergencies

Parents and caregivers benefit from understanding basic emergency response and considering CPR training.
