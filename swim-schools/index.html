What Makes a 5-Star Swim School?

🛡️
Safety-First Approach
Certified lifeguards, small class sizes, and constant supervision.
👨‍🏫
Qualified Instructors
WSI-certified or equivalent training with years of experience.
👥
Small Class Sizes
6–8 students per instructor for personalized attention.
📈
Clear Progression
Well-defined levels from beginner to advanced swimmer.
💬
Parent Communication
Regular feedback and progress updates from instructors.
