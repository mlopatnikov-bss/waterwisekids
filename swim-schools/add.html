Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my school to appear?
Most schools appear in our directory within 24-48 business hours. We review all submissions to ensure accuracy and quality.
Can I upgrade to a Featured Listing later?
Absolutely. You can start with a free listing and upgrade to Featured ($49/month) anytime through your account dashboard.
What if I have multiple locations?
Submit a separate form for each location. Each gets its own profile. Contact us about multi-location pricing.
Can I edit my listing after it's live?
Yes. You'll have access to an account dashboard where you can update your hours, programs, description, and contact info anytime.
Do you charge a commission on inquiries?
No. You only pay the listing fee (if you upgrade). We don't take a cut of your business. Every inquiry is yours to keep.
What information will be public?
Your school name, location, phone, website, age groups, programs, and description will be public. Your email is private and only shown to parents who submit the contact form.
Can I remove my listing later?
Of course. Contact us anytime to pause or delete your listing. No penalty.
