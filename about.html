About WaterWiseKids

WaterWiseKids is a national water safety resource built to help parents make better decisions around swim lessons, water confidence, and child safety in and around water.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make water safety easier for families to understand and act on. We publish practical articles, educational resources, and swim lesson discovery tools that help parents protect their children and build confidence in the water.

What We Cover

WaterWiseKids focuses on topics like when children should start swim lessons, how to teach float skills, pool safety rules for families, and how to find quality swim programs near you.

Why This Site Exists

Parents already search online for answers about water safety and swim lessons. WaterWiseKids is designed to become a trusted place for those answers while also helping families discover strong swim instruction options.
