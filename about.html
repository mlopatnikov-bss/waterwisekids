About WaterWiseKids

A parent-focused guide to water safety, swim readiness, and confident swimmers.

Our Mission

WaterWiseKids exists to help families make smart, confident decisions about water safety, swim lessons, drowning prevention, and long-term swimmer development.

We believe children should grow up not only enjoying the water, but also respecting it, understanding it, and learning the skills that can help keep them safe.

What We Share

WaterWiseKids publishes practical information for parents on topics such as:

  • when kids should start swim lessons
  • how children learn to float
  • water safety rules for families
  • helping nervous children feel more confident in water
  • swim lessons for children with special abilities
  • strong swimmer development and endurance programs
  • lifeguard certification and aquatic training opportunities

Who Operates WaterWiseKids

WaterWiseKids is operated by VTL Holding LLC and is designed as a family-friendly educational resource for parents who want trustworthy guidance around water safety and swim education.

The site is informed by real-world experience in swim instruction, water safety education, swimmer development, and family-focused aquatic programming.

Local Swim Lesson Resources

For families in Northwest Philadelphia and the Jersey Shore, WaterWiseKids also highlights local swim lesson opportunities, scholarship-supported access, and swimmer development options.

Featured local areas include Cheltenham, Andorra, Howell, Brick, Toms River, and Asbury Park.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple: help more children become safer, stronger, and more confident around water.
