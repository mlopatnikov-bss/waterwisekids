About the project

A modern water safety resource built for parents, not for industry insiders.

WaterWiseKids exists to make swim lessons, water confidence, and child water safety easier to understand. The mission is simple: give families better guidance and a cleaner path to action.

Mission

Make water safety easier to understand and act on.

Parents often search for answers about swim readiness, fear of water, floating, lesson frequency, and drowning prevention. This site is designed to become a trusted place for those answers.

Approach

Simple guidance, strong structure, and useful next steps.

Instead of burying families in jargon, WaterWiseKids focuses on plain-English education, clean layout, and easy navigation into local swim lesson pages.

What WaterWiseKids covers

Water safety basics

Supervision, safer habits, floating, confidence, and drowning prevention.

Swim lesson education

What to look for in a program, when to start, and how to help beginners succeed.

Lesson discovery

Location pages by city, county, and stage so parents can move from reading to action.
